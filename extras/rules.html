// 0x01 General Rules

  • Honor Code Compliance: Participants must adhere to the **Honor Code** detailed below.
  • No Cheating: Participants must not engage in any form of cheating, including but not limited to exploiting vulnerabilities, sharing flags or solutions, or collaborating with others outside their team.
  • Infrastructure Integrity: Do not attack or disrupt the competition infrastructure. If you discover any vulnerabilities, report them immediately to the organizers.
  • Respect: Respect other participants and their efforts. Any form of harassment, abuse, or unsportsmanlike conduct will not be tolerated.
  • Final Decisions: The decision of the organizers regarding any disputes or rule violations is final.
  • Eligibility for Prizes: Participants who are currently studying in school/college are eligible for prizes and must provide proof if they win. Even if you are not a student, you can participate but will not be eligible for prizes.
  • Team Limit: Team limit is set to 4 members.
  • Have fun and good luck!
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

// 0x02 Honor Code

  • I will play fairly and honestly, without cheating or exploiting.
  • I will respect the rules and guidelines set forth by the organizers.
  • I will not engage in any actions that may harm the competition or its participants.
  • I will uphold the principles of sportsmanship and integrity throughout the competition.
